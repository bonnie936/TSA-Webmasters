Community Resource Directory

Johnson County Public Library

The Johnson County Public Library offers a wide range of resources including books, digital media, and educational programs for all ages.

Johnson County Chamber of Commerce

A local organization in Paintsville, Kentucky that supports and promotes businesses in the community.

Johnson County Health Department

The Johnson County Health Department website is the official online presence for the public health department serving Paintsville and Johnson County, Kentucky, providing information about their health services, programs, and community resources.

Johnson County Clerk Office

The Johnson County Clerk Office provides residents with access to a variety of government services including records, voter registration, and motor vehicle licensing.

Food Pantries

NEEDS TO BE EDITED! This website shows nearby food pantries in Johnson County, helping those experiencing food insecurity.

Warming Center

The Warming Center page for Johnson County provides information about emergency cold weather shelter available to homeless residents and anyone in need during extreme winter temperatures in Paintsville.

Johnson County Official Website

This is the official website for Johnson County, Kentucky, serving as a central hub for county government information, resident services, elected officials, emergency management, and visitor resources for Paintsville and the surrounding area.

4H Extension Office

The Johnson County 4-H program is a youth development organization operated through the University of Kentucky Cooperative Extension Service, dedicated to building leadership, life skills, and community engagement in young people across Johnson County, Kentucky.
