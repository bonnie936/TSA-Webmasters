Project Purpose

The Johnson County Community Resource Hub is dedicated to connecting residents with the support and opportunities they need to thrive. We believe that everyone deserves access to resources that can help them overcome challenges and improve their quality of life. This platform highlights the strong networks of collaboration, support, and civic engagement that define our community.

Explore the Resource Directory

How It Works

  • The homepage showcases community highlights for quick access.
  • Residents can browse a curated collection of local resources organized by category.
  • New resources can be submitted to expand the directory over time.

Project Goals

  • Highlight important community resources.
  • Provide an accessible platform for residents to find local support services.
  • Encourage community engagement and collaboration among residents and organizations.
